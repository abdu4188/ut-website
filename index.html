About

UT solutions PLC

UT Solutions PLC is a professional service based company originally from Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. UT Solutions PLC specializes in providing all-rounded IT solutions for businesses ranging from local to international, small to corporate status, Private to Governmental. We have been providing quality services in Networking, Server and Backup, PBX, Telephony, technology traininngs and ERPs for the past six years. We have partnered with international tech giants Cisco, Microsoft, ManageEngine, Veeam, HP and many others.
